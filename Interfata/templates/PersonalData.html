WHAT ARE PERSONAL DATA?
"Personal data" means any information or information that can identify you directly (for example, your name) or indirectly (for example, through pseudonymous data, such as a unique identification number). This means that personal data includes things like email / home address / mobile phone, username, profile pictures, personal preferences and shopping habits, user-generated content, financial information and financial statement information. It may also include unique numeric identifiers, such as the IP address of your computer or the MAC address of your mobile device, as well as cookies.
